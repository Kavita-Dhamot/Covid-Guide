What Are The Main Symptoms?

CDC will continue to update this list as we learn more about COVID-19. Older adults and people who have severe underlying medical conditions like heart or lung disease or diabetes seem to be at higher risk for developing more serious complications from COVID-19 illness.

things not to do during covid

By following good respiratory hygiene you protect the people around you from viruses that cause colds, flu and COVID-19.

  • Do Not Share Eating
  • Do Not Touch Your Face or Nose
  • Do Not Contact Sick People

things to do during covid

By following good respiratory hygiene you protect the people around you from viruses that cause colds, flu and COVID-19.

  • Wash Your Hands For 20 sec
  • Wear a Mask if Available
  • Seek Medical Care Regularly
